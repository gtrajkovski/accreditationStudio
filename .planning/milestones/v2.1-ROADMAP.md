# v2.1 Roadmap — Commercial Readiness

**Goal:** Transform AccreditAI from a single-user localhost tool into a multi-user, commercially viable product ready for its first paying customers.

**Target customers:**
- ACCSC-accredited career schools (~650 institutions)
- Accreditation consultants (managing multiple institutions)

**Strategic position:** AccreditAI is the **only AI-native product** in the accreditation management market. Competitors (Watermark, SPOL, Weave, Anthology, Nuventive) provide workflow/data management but no document-level AI auditing, auto-remediation, or autonomous packet assembly.

## Blockers Addressed

| Blocker | Solution | Phase |
|---------|----------|-------|
| No multi-user support | User auth + RBAC | 41-42 |
| No onboarding experience | 4-step wizard | 46 |
| No accountability trail | Activity logging | 43 |

Cloud deployment deferred to v2.2.
